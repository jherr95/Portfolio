Restaurant Description

This fine esablishment was founded by a group of college kids that while in school realized the lack of, personal and social,
time spent in the kitchen. They quickly developed a cooking acadamy that transended their dream in school and this lead them down the
next logical path after school which was to open up a restaurant. The very first Jay Boys Cooking restaurant opened just 6 monthes after
they graduated. It is a unique spot not just because of their wildly creative menu and inviting and infectious attmosphere, but also the
experience where you can join in the action heating up in the kitchen. This has quickly become the hottest spot in the area...
Especially for date nights!
